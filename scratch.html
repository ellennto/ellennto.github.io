Catch the Snowflakes!

Who does not like Christmas? That was the question that popped in our minds, when we were
thinking about the theme. We both wanted to create a game, in which you could enjoy and get
yourself into the Christmas spirit. Also, in the game, you move the arrow keys to catch
the snowflakes. You earn points when you catch the snowflakes and lose points when you catch
the skates. There are only two levels, and it gets harder.

Describe some programming concepts or structures you learned in this unit that you applied in your game.

Some programming concepts that I've learned in this unit is that in coding, every little thing counts. It
is very percise. Knowing that, I made sure to run the play everytime I made a change to ensure the smoothness.

No, I didn't really encounter problems working on this project! :)

What aspects of your game do you like best?

I think I like level 2's structure and layout because it is more interesting and pretty.

If more time was given for you to work on your game, what would you improve or modify about your game?

If extra time were to be given on this game, I would add some sound in it, and make it run smoother; go over
