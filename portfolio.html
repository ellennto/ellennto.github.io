The interactive fiction that my partner and I have created is about two guys who fall in love with
the same girl, Isabella. Little do the guys know, Isabella has a boyfriend and when the she befriends them,
she thought of them as friends. The story consists of 3 options that will lead to a sad ending.
Click the link above to play the story!

consistently check our coding if it returned an error. I also learned that coding isn't only for making websites. I didn't
know that you could make it interactive. Also, all the notes and examples that I've done in class, I utilized
the repl.it notes to construct the story.
